title Volunteer Sign-up Flow

VolunteerSignUp.razor->VolunteerDataService: Save Request
VolunteerSignUp.razor->SignUpDataService: Create Sign-up
VolunteerSignUp.razor->EmailBuilderService: Sign-up Confirmation Email
EmailBuilderService->ContentDataService: Get Email Tempalte
note right of EmailBuilderService: Build Sign-up Confirmation Body
EmailBuilderService->EmailQueueDataService: Queue Email
VolunteerSignUp.razor->SendSmsLogic: Create Sign-up SMS Reminder
SendSmsLogic->ContentDataService: Get SMS Tempalte
note right of SendSmsLogic: Build Queue Record
SendSmsLogic->SmsQueueDataService: Queue SMS
